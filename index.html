What is Nurse Wellness and Self-Care

Nurses wellness and self-care means taking care of yourself -- your body, your mind, and your feelings -- so you can stay healthy, happy and strong at work and in life. Nurses work hard everyday to care for others. But sometimes, they forget to care for themselves. Self-care is about making time for you, even if it's just for a few minutes.

Why is it important?

When nurses take care of themselves, they can feel less tired and stressed, think clearly and make better decisions, be more positive and calm, most especially they will be able to help patients better.
